Welcome to the latest technology in Medicine.

Our web app simplifies your healthcare experience by sending mobile SMS notifications for doctor appointments and medical prescriptions. With our convenient service, you'll receive timely reminders for your upcoming appointments, ensuring you never miss an important visit. Additionally, we securely deliver your medical prescriptions directly to your mobile device, saving you time and effort. Experience hassle-free healthcare management with our SMS-based solution.

What We Do

Doctor Appointment SMS Notification

Never miss a doctor's appointment again! Our web app sends SMS notifications to remind you of your upcoming appointments, ensuring you stay on track with your healthcare schedule.

Medicine Prescription SMS Notification

Say goodbye to forgotten prescriptions. We deliver your medical prescriptions through SMS, providing a convenient and secure way to access your medications whenever you need them.

Health Check-Up Notification

Take control of your health. Our web app sends you regular SMS notifications to remind you of upcoming health check-ups, helping you stay proactive in maintaining your well-being.
